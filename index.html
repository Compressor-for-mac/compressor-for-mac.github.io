How It Works

Step 1: Import and Preview

Drag files into Compressor. Use the Preview window for real-time feedback on settings adjustments.

Step 2: Select or Customize Settings

Choose from the library or build custom presets for codecs, formats, and parameters. Add filters, markers, or metadata as needed.

Step 3: Batch and Distribute

Set up batches or Droplets. Enable distributed encoding across your cluster for accelerated processing.

Step 4: Export and Deliver

Output to any format, disc, or streaming service. Presets sync back to Final Cut Pro for seamless reuse.

FAQ

Does it integrate with Final Cut Pro?

Yes. Custom settings created in Compressor appear directly in Final Cut Pro exports.

What's required for distributed encoding?

Install Compressor on each Mac in your cluster. Use QuickCluster for automatic node management based on resources.

Can I share presets with others?

Absolutely. Presets work in Final Cut Pro even on machines without Compressor.

Is this the full Compressor 5.0?

This is the fully activated version—no purchases or activations needed. Ready to use immediately on your Mac.
